## Introduction

The AgroSoluce platform now includes comprehensive agricultural health framework compliance features designed to help cooperatives track, analyze, and report on workforce health indicators that directly impact agricultural productivity.

### Key Features

- **Framework Compliance Tracking**: Visual indicators showing alignment with agricultural health framework standards
- **Expanded Health Indicators**: Beyond malaria to include nutrition, occupational safety, healthcare access, and preventive care
- **Framework-Compliant Reports**: Export standardized reports that meet official framework requirements
- **Real-Time Analytics**: Interactive dashboards showing health trends and correlations with agricultural productivity
- **Multi-Stakeholder Support**: Role-based access for farmers, assessors, administrators, and partners

## Getting Started

### Accessing Health Impact Features

1. **Navigate to Health & Impact**: From the main menu, select "Health & Impact" or visit `/health-impact`
2. **View Framework Compliance**: Check the Framework Compliance Badge at the top of the page
3. **Explore Health Indicators**: Access the comprehensive health indicators dashboard
4. **Generate Reports**: Use the Export Report feature to create framework-compliant documentation

### User Roles

- **Farmers**: View health metrics for their cooperative, understand health-productivity correlations
- **Assessors**: Input health data, generate assessment reports, track compliance
- **Administrators**: Manage framework version tracking, configure indicators, oversee compliance
- **Partners**: Access aggregated health data for research and partnership opportunities

## Exporting Reports

### Report Types

#### 1. Summary Report
- **Best For**: Quick overviews, executive presentations
- **Contents**: Key health metrics, status summaries, trend highlights
- **Typical Use**: Monthly reviews, stakeholder updates

#### 2. Detailed Report
- **Best For**: Comprehensive analysis, in-depth reviews
- **Contents**: Full health indicator data, trend analysis, correlations with productivity
- **Typical Use**: Quarterly assessments, operational planning

#### 3. Framework-Compliant Report
- **Best For**: Official documentation, certification, external audits
- **Contents**: All framework-required indicators, standardized format, compliance documentation
- **Typical Use**: Annual reporting, certification processes, partner requirements

### Export Formats

- **PDF**: Formatted reports, best for sharing and printing
- **Excel**: Spreadsheets with data for further analysis
- **CSV**: Raw data for import into other systems

### How to Export

1. Click the **"Export Report"** button on any health dashboard page
2. Select your **Report Type** (Summary, Detailed, or Framework-Compliant)
3. Choose your **Export Format** (PDF, Excel, or CSV)
4. Click **"Export Report"** to generate
5. Download automatically begins when ready

## Understanding Health Metrics

### The Health-Agriculture Correlation

Research shows a strong correlation between workforce health and agricultural productivity:

- **Malaria Impact**: 40-60% reduction in harvest efficiency during acute malaria episodes
- **Time Lag**: Health impacts typically show in production data 3-4 weeks later
- **Early Warning**: Pharmacy surveillance provides advance notice of health challenges

### Key Metrics Explained

#### Antimalarial Sales Index
- **What**: Tracks sales of antimalarial medications at regional pharmacies
- **Why**: Serves as real-time proxy for malaria burden in workforce
- **Interpretation**: Higher index = greater disease burden = potential productivity impact
- **Trend**: Declining index indicates improving health conditions

#### Nutrition Program Participation
- **What**: Percentage of cooperative families enrolled in nutrition support programs
- **Why**: Proper nutrition is fundamental to workforce health and productivity
- **Target**: 80%+ participation recommended
- **Trend**: Increasing participation improves long-term health outcomes

#### Safety Incidents
- **What**: Number of reported occupational health and safety incidents
- **Why**: Tracks workplace safety and identifies risk areas
- **Target**: Zero incidents; any trend upward requires immediate attention
- **Note**: Increased reporting initially is positive (better awareness)

3. **Support Data Quality**: Provide training on proper health data collection

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "Framework Compliance shows 'Review Needed'"
- **Cause**: Missing critical health indicators or outdated data
- **Solution**: Check which indicators are missing, submit pending data, contact administrator

#### "Cannot export reports"
- **Cause**: Incomplete data, browser issues, or insufficient permissions
- **Solution**: Verify data completeness, try different browser, check user role permissions

#### "Health indicators not updating"
- **Cause**: Data submission delay or processing lag
- **Solution**: Refresh page, allow 24 hours for processing, contact support if persistent

#### "Don't understand a health metric"
- **Cause**: Unfamiliar framework terminology
- **Solution**: Hover over metric names for definitions, consult this guide, contact assessor
