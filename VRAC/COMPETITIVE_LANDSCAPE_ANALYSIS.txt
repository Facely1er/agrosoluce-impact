EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

YES, there ARE multiple public and commercial solutions providing weather and crop conditions data for Côte d'Ivoire and West African cocoa regions with weekly or even daily granularity.

However, none of these solutions track the HUMAN HEALTH component that directly affects harvest efficiency. This is your differentiated value proposition.
