MECHANISM ESTABLISHED IN LITERATURE
================================================================================

How malaria reduces agricultural output:

1. DIRECT ABSENTEEISM
   Workers too sick to go to fields during critical periods
   
2. REDUCED CAPACITY  
   Working while ill or recovering = lower productivity
   
3. DELAYED OPERATIONS
   "Farmers affected by malaria end up ploughing, sowing, weeding, harvesting 
   LATE, which results in low productivity" (Ghana study)
   
4. CAREGIVING TIME
   Healthy adults care for sick family members instead of farming

5. VICIOUS CYCLE
   Malaria → low harvest → food insecurity → malnutrition → weaker immune 
   system → more malaria

All of these mechanisms are peer-reviewed and documented.
